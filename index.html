Fintech · Wealth Management Live

Personal Wealth Management AI Agent

A hybrid AI agent that combines a structured client financial profile with live market research to deliver personalized, actionable wealth management guidance. The agent autonomously decides when to search for current market data, cross-references it with the client's goals, risk tolerance, and timeline, and proactively flags misalignments in allocation strategy.
